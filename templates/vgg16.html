VGG16 – Convolutional Network for Classification and Detection



The model achieves 92.7% top-5 test accuracy in ImageNet, which is a dataset of over 14 million images belonging to 1000 classes. And the model has 16 convolutional layers with small receptive fields (3x3), 144 million parameters, five max- pooling layers (2x2 size) and three fully connected layers, with a soft-max activation feature on the final layer. The input to cov1 layer is of fixed size 224 x 224 RGB image. The image is passed through a stack of convolutional layers, where the filters were used with a very small receptive field: 3×3.
